OVERVIEW

Sandboard is a PC VR sand surfing and grappling game for Oculus Quest (2) and HTC Vive. With a sandboard at your feet and two grapple hands at your disposal, zip down sandy slopes, fly off boosts, and use your grapples to propel yourself through the Arverre badlands.

RECEPTION

This project received a 1st mark, earned my place as an ImmerseUK Awards finalist in the Best Overall category, was selected for the 2023 Aesthetica Short Film Festival, and won the Spotlight Award at the 2022 University of York Interactive Media Showcase.
