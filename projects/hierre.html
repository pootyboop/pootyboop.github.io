ⓘ OVERVIEW

Hierre is a sci-fi star system and consistent universe I have been developing across a range of projects. It is set in the future, where a lucky colony has occupied a paradise system and struggles to create a new society. As the young Hierri colony spreads across Hierre's 7 habitable planets, it faces challenges such as civil war, dangerous fauna, and mysterious storms.

The Hierre system is heavily documented in an up-to-date Google Doc which includes tons of info about planets, fauna, culture, language, and history.

☆ FUN FACTS

  • Hierre was initially inspired by Isaac Asimov's Foundation and 1700s America
  • There are 10 major languages with unique auditory identities and conventions that reflects their culture
  • Hierri count in base-16 and count years from the date they arrived in the system
  • I have a mini 3D printed sandboard and a Kallay Tirridor hiring advert poster in my room
