ⓘ OVERVIEW

I was on the Design team for the 2024 Interactive Media Showcase, a student work showcase ran by students on my course.

I also submitted some projects to this showcase: Gata Guressi, CONDUCTOR, and Sky's the Limit.

⌨ DEVELOPMENT

I made a promotional animation in Blender, which included modelling and animating the 2024 showcase mascot, rendering out the animation, and adding final touches in Premiere.

I also rendered the logo hand in Blender, which we then used to make the final logo.
