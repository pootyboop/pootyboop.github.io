Aside from work on the Sandboard 1.1 update, there were three main projects I developed while at XR Stories (listed in chronological order):


AR Guildhall

  • AR Android app displaying different digital elements around the York Guildhall made in Unity
  • Created 3D models of pieces of York history such as the York Minster and the York coat of arms
  • Vuforia image tracking identifies activation points such as windows or plaques in the Guildhall
  • Made in collaboration with Ziqi Fang

VR CML

  • VR replica of the XR Stories lab created for a 3D scanning tech demo
  • Interactive level featuring swinging light fixtures, openable cabinets, and destructible computer monitors

Prox & Reverie Mockup

  • Virtual production mockup set for preparing future film shoots made in Unreal Engine 5
  • Configurable lighting, characters, and scenes following guidelines from film production companies Prox & Reverie and Bridgeway
  • Real-time configurable camera with film backs, focal length, aperture, and focus distance
  • Made in collaboration with Ziqi Fang
