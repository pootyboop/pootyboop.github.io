The Hitz Hotel is a 2D puzzle/stealth game where you must kill targets without getting caught. Murder your way across a chain hotel's various locations, hiding targets' bodies and avoiding innocent guests.

I developed this project with Joe Hutchinson for Brackeys Game Jam 2024.1. I created the art and levels for the game and Joe managed tech.
