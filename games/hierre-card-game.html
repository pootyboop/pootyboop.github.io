ⓘ OVERVIEW

This is a grid-based card game I've got on the backburner. It plays like a mix of chess, Magic: The Gathering, and Inscryption (the game's biggest inspiration).

⌨ DEVELOPMENT

This is a personal project. I create models in Blender, render in Unreal Engine, and create the cards in Photoshop. I have an online multiplayer Unity adaptation in very early stages, but haven't had much time to continue development.
