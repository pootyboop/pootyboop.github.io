ⓘ OVERVIEW

DOBIO is a procedural dungeon-building deck builder in which the player takes control of the dungeon itself in order to carve out a path for the protagonist, Lance. Find treasure to raise your vigor, luck and insight in order to overcome obstacles and defeat the boss (DEFUNCT) of each floor. Use your hand wisely and clear as many floors as you can!

This project was created for the GMTK 2023 Game Jam under the theme: roles reversed. It plays on the typical dungeon crawler by reversing the player's role in the game: rather than controlling the protagonist, they control the dungeon. You are not the crawler, you are the dungeon. DOBIO.
