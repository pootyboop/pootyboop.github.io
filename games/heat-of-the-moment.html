Heat of the Moment is an intense, fast-paced game where you must make quick decisions to escape a burning house. As you pass through scenes, you will find items that open up new options and encounter characters like the arsonist and the assassin.

Each run will result in one of 21 possible endings. The game is highly replayable and I encourage replaying it many times to see how many different endings you can get. It also features a cross-game storyline unlocking new items, characters, scenes, and endings.

üëÅ RECEPTION

This project earned a 1st mark for its nonlinear story, item management puzzles, and scene parsing system facilitating modding. It won the Audience Favorite Award at the 2022 University of York Interactive Media Showcase.

The game also got some coverage from itch.io YouTubers MrDreaming, Laudplays, and Levont.

üñ≥ DEVELOPMENT

This was a 1st year university project developed over the course of 3 months in Processing. Scenes were rendered in Blender, pixel art was made in Aseprite, and sound effects were made in Logic Pro (mostly with Massive) . It was primarily inspired by early dungeon crawlers, Ape Out's art style, and narrative in games like Katana ZERO.
