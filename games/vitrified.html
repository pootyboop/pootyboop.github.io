Vitrified is a fast-paced FPS where you must use specific potions to beat waves of enemies. A mysterious virus has ravaged your village, causing civilians to mutate into animalistic crystal creatures. Each enemy behaves differently and can only be defeated by 1 of your 3 distinctive potion types. Survive as many waves as possible with quick thinking and strategic play!

We developed the game in Unity using Blender for 3D models, Photoshop for textures and UI, and Studio One for audio. Vitrified draws inspiration from Pokemon's type system, movement shooters like Titanfall 2, and the aesthetic of games like Bloodborne, Darkest Dungeon, and Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery.
