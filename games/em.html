ⓘ OVERVIEW

EM was a 2D story-based platforming and exploration game. I lost the game files to a hard drive failure, so only concept art and a handful of videos remain.

This game is loosely set in the Hierre system and was the first rendition of planets Ronetto and Lunscu.

⌨ DEVELOPMENT

EM was made in Unity before switching to Godot 3. This was my first foray into full-fledged game development back in 2018-19. Some of this game's soundtrack was adapted and released in my first album, Onion Ecdysis.
