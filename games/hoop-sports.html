Hoop Sports is a top-down sports game with annoyingly bouncy characters. Slam into the ball and other players in 3 different game modes: football, bumpers (bump enemies into the goal), and dodgeball. Play at your chosen difficulty or spectate games of up to 256 AI players duking it out.

This project received a 1st mark for its physics system and AI behaviors (BSc Interactive Media, University of York). It was also featured in the 2022 University of York Interactive Media Showcase.

üñ≥ DEVELOPMENT

This is a showcase of a physics system I created and shoehorned into a 1st year university project. Built entirely in Processing, the project features extremely irritating sound effects beautifully complimented by the distortion of Processing's buggy sound library. I coded bouncing and sliding physics with pixel-accurate collision to enable fun interactions between players.
