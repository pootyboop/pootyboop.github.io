Soundtracks 2021-24 is a compilation of soundtracks I produced for three university projects: Sandboard, Head in the Clouds, and Gata Guressi.

ðŸŽ¹ PRODUCTION

I produced all soundtracks in Logic Pro using Massive, Alchemy and trap drumkits.

You can listen to the latest full-project bounce as of 24 June 2024 above.

The Head in the Clouds soundtrack consists of a collection of tracks that fade in and out depending on in-game conditions. The version in the soundtrack compilation plays all tracks simultaneously.

"Pami Tir" from Gata Guressi is the full, non-looping version of the song the band Bila Gola plays in Kallay Tirridor. I added crowd noise in the background and panned each instrument to make the song sound like a live rendition.
