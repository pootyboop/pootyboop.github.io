Mind of Glass revolves around phobias. It works with a mix of synths and real-world samples, differing from Saliva by trying to evoke anxiety rather than wonder. It draws heavy inspiration from Flying Lotus, Black Midi, G Jones, and Amon Tobin alongside Billy Woods, Madlib, and other electronica and old-school hip hop artists.

Mind of Glass is my 3rd LP.
